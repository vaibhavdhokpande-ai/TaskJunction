RC / LC Filter Calculator

Cutoff frequency, impedance and phase for RC low-pass, high-pass and LC bandpass filters with Bode plot preview.

📉 RC Low-Pass Filter
Passes low frequencies, attenuates high frequencies. Roll-off: −20 dB/decade.
📊 Bode Plot — Magnitude Response
Cutoff Freq: fc = 1 / (2π × R × C)
Gain at f: |H| = 1 / √(1 + (f/fc)²)
Phase: φ = −arctan(f/fc)
📈 RC High-Pass Filter
Passes high frequencies, blocks low frequencies. Roll-off: +20 dB/decade below fc.
📊 Bode Plot — Magnitude Response
Cutoff Freq: fc = 1 / (2π × R × C)
Gain at f: |H| = (f/fc) / √(1 + (f/fc)²)
Phase: φ = 90° − arctan(f/fc)
📻 LC Band-Pass Filter
Resonant circuit passing a band of frequencies around resonant frequency fr.
Resonant Freq: fr = 1 / (2π√(LC))
Q Factor: Q = fr × L / R = 1/(R√(C/L))
Bandwidth: BW = fr / Q
⚡ RLC Series Circuit
Complete RLC series circuit analysis — impedance, current and power factor at given frequency.
XL = 2πfL, XC = 1/(2πfC)
Z = √(R² + (XL−XC)²)
Power Factor = R/Z = cos(φ)
