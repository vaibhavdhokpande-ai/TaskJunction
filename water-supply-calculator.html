Hazen-Williams Formula
Pressure drop calculation

Hazen-Williams Equation

V = 0.8492 · C · R^0.63 · S^0.54
where R = hydraulic radius (D/4), S = slope
Q = V × A

Head Loss

hf = 10.67 · L · Q^1.852 / (C^1.852 · D^4.87)
Q in m³/s, D in m, L in m

Balancing Storage

Required to balance hourly variation when
supply rate ≠ demand rate over the day.
Typically 15–30% of ADD.

Total Storage

= Balancing Storage
+ Fire Fighting Reserve
+ Emergency Reserve
+ Freeboard volume
