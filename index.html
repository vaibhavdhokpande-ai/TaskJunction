Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

TaskJunction does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account or log in.

All tools run locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or share any user data on our servers.

3. Permissions Used

  • Internet Access: Required to load tools and resources.

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

Our app and website are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
